# Dorna Design System

**Product:** Dorna is a calm, modern, audio-first daily English companion for Iranian newcomers in Canada. It helps people have real conversations in everyday life.

**Feel:** trustworthy, warm, encouraging, premium.

## Color
- Primary blue #1C75BC (buttons, active, play). Pressed #285F8E.
- Accent cyan #05C1E2 (waveforms, highlights, secondary actions).
- Sky/info #039BE5.
- Background #F9F9F9; secondary surface #F2F2F2; cards #FFFFFF 16px radius soft shadow.
- Text #232E36 / #57585A / #76787B.
- Success #17BD62, Warning #FF9500, Error #D80027. Persian hint blocks #EDEDED.

## Signature
- Daily audio brief hero and primary play button use a blue-to-cyan gradient (#1C75BC to #05C1E2). Cyan waveform motif for audio.

## Type and layout
- Inter font. Mobile 390x844, ONE viewport per screen, no long page scroll; overflow into tabs/segments/carousels. Large tap targets. Single home hub, no 5-tab bottom bar. Persian may appear in content areas (RTL).

## Avoid
Teal, neon gradients, cartoon mascots, cluttered dashboards, stock learn-English clipart, 5-tab navigation.
